Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how LookList ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website and app. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

How do we use cookies?
  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function, including security and authentication.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website, enabling us to improve functionality.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver ads relevant to you and to measure their effectiveness.
Your choices

You can choose to accept or refuse cookies. Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your experience on our platform.

Changes to this policy

We may update our Cookies Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.
